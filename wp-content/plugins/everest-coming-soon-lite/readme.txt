Everything you wish for your coming soon page is now possible with <strong>Everest Coming Soon Lite – an ultimate WordPress coming soon plugin</strong>.  Whenever your site is on maintenance mode or under construction, make it smooth and pleasing to your visitors. The plugin is packaged with <strong>2 beautifully pre-designed coming soon page templates</strong>, choose any and create the most appealing maintenance pages faster than ever.  It is a highly flexible plugin which authorize you full control to configure most of its components as per your preference.

<strong>Everest Coming Soon</strong> has included the entire possibilities, that you could have thought of. It allows you to embed countdown timer, contact form, subscription form and much more. Beside, you can personalize your coming soon page with custom logo, custom background,  social links, quick infos, and so on. Do your work in private and make it pleasing and professional-looking to others.

== Installation ==
1. Unzip everest-coming-soon-lite.zip
1. Upload all the files to the /wp-content/plugins/everest-coming-soon-lite
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. For customizing the plugin's settings, click on Everest Coming Soon Lite option menu in Wordpress left admin menu.
1. Enable maintenance mode from general settings section of the Everest Coming Soon Lite options.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= I have enabled the maintenance mode but still I can't view it, why? =

 If even after enabling the maintenance mode, you are still unable to view the maintenance mode page, then you are probably logged in with administrator role. So to view maintenance mode page, you will need to logout or view the site in browser's private or incognito mode.
